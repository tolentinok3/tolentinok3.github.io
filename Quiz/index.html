PICK UP YOUR TOOLS.

Five essential tools optimized for the creative process to Sketch, Write, Draw, Outline and Color. Start with Draw and purchase additional tools from in-app store.

Draw

Captures ideas beautifully.
This versatile fountain pen draws from thick to thin based on your movement. It's quintessential drawing tool for any idea.

Sketch

Start with a sketch.
This soft lead pencil blends from light to dark for sketching scenes, objects, and ideas in rough form.

Outline

Think bold.
Graph insights, draft a plan, or outline a presentation with this cold marker.

Write

The power of words.
Write messages, add captions, or capture your thoughts with this quality ink pen. The written word never looked so beautiful.

Color

Give color to your ideas.
Master watercolor as you move from a light wash to deep, rich color with the speed of the brush as colors blend naturally on the page.

Mixer

Play between the shades.
A new, natural way to mix beautiful colors and create your own custom palettes.
